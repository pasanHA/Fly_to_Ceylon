Located in Nuwara Eliya, 2.4 km from Gregory Lake, The Ramp provides accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking and a garden. The property is around 9 km from Hakgala Botanical Garden, 2 km from Galway's Land National Park and 3.2 km from Single Tree Hill. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests. Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a wardrobe. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, certain rooms at the hotel also offer a patio. A continental breakfast is available daily at The Ramp. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Victoria Park of Nuwara Eliya, Nuwara Eliya Post Office and Mackwoods Museum. Couples particularly like the location.
