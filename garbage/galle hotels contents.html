Located in Galle Fort, a world heritage site, The Bartizan’s original structure was built in the early 17th century Dutch colonial period. The building has undergone careful renovations to restore the structure to its former glory. Situated alongside the fort’s perimeter, The Bartizan has a rooftop terrace and unique tower rooms that offer unsurpassable views of the ocean and Galle Fort.

Located in the historic fort city of Galle on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, Amari Galle is a luxury resort featuring rooms and suites, an outdoor pool with a pool bar, spa and fitness centre. All rooms boast of a direct view of the Indian Ocean, carefully designed to offer living spaces with a 40-inch LED TV, complimentary Wi-Fi, work desk, mini bar, tea and coffee making and ironing facilities. The bathrooms offer a rain shower, complimentary toiletries, bathrobe and bath slippers and hair dryers. AHARA Gourmet Gallery is Amari Galle’s signature all-day dining restaurant serving a buffet breakfast. A range of Asian and International cuisine is prepared from live cooking stations using only fresh and hand-picked produce, and is available for lunch and dinner. BOMMU Rooftop Bar offers a panoramic view of the surroundings along with cocktails and finger foods.
